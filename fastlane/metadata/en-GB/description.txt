Highway Story is drag racing strory game where perfect shifts take you through the roads between cities.Start from humble beginning with one of the starting cars, go race, learn fast: shift on time to win, wins earns your keep and let you upgrade your ride! Beat stages and bosses to unlock new harder levels, wheels, and new cars.

Play the story with three episodesm, each with it's own stages and bosses. Click Race to open a map of 15 stages split across connected routes. Each 5th stage is a boss showdown and each stage gives you a small story beat for you to enjoy. 

To achieve victory you need to upgrade your car in the garage, power, speed, grip and weight are key parts of making your ride faster. If you feel like you hit a limit - buy a new car, and upgrade it even further. 

Play one of two modes story or arcade,  complete Achievements, and most importantly have fun!
